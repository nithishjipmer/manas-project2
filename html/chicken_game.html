Are the chickens both side same?

How to play?
Same-or-not Game
  1. Two sets of three chicken images will shown on left and right side of the screen for a brief period of time
  2. Then the user has to decide whether the group of images on the right is exactly same as left or not and choose the options accordingly
  3. Each correct answer fetches 10 points and wrong answer costs a life
  4. The game ends in one minute or with a wrong answer (whichever occurs first)
